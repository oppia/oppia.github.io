The Exploration Gallery

After signing in to Oppia, you'll end up on the Home page. Even before you start creating your own explorations, there are a number of things you can do. You can click on the about icon at the top right corner of the page (for large screen sizes) or the icon at the top left corner (for small screen sizes) to access the blog, join the discussion forum or access the teach with oppia and other options. You can also check out a variety of existing explorations in different categories, or create one of your own by clicking on "Create Exploration" at the top right.

for other screen sizes

The LIBRARY contains a list of all published, non-draft explorations on the server. The explorations are grouped in various categories that they best fit in. Example, the quadratic equation is found under the category "Mathematematics and statistics".

You can filter the list by searching for specific topics, or selecting particular categories and languages. (You can also change the default language settings on your Preferences page.) In the following screenshot, the user has selected the "Mathematics and statistics" category, so the gallery shows only mathematics and statistics explorations.

To play an exploration, click anywhere on its gallery tile. If you have editing permissions, an "Edit" option will also appear in the top right of its tile.
